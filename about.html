About Fortuna

Boutique planning, personal service.

Fortuna Events is a founder-led firm that plans and produces corporate events with the attention to detail that busy executives expect and deserve.

Our Story

Est. 2018

Fortuna Events was founded eight years ago on a simple idea: busy executives should not have to stress about event logistics.

They need a partner who handles everything, from the venue to the table settings to the day-of coordination, so they can focus on their guests and their message.

We work with senior leaders, founders, and organizations who value quality and discretion. Our clients expect white-glove service, and that is exactly what we deliver: one dedicated planner, obsessive attention to detail, and seamless execution.

Every engagement is personally led by our founding team. Every detail is accounted for. And every event we produce is measured by one thing: whether you and your guests had an exceptional experience.
